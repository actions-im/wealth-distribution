The Federal Reserve’s 2022 Survey of Consumer Finances public files used here contain **4,595 surveyed SCF families** represented in **five multiple-imputation implicates** (**22,975 record rows**). The report applies the supplied SCF family weight **WGT** to produce weighted family totals—not person counts. It combines the summary file’s NETWORTH and WGT with the detailed file’s respondent/spouse demographics, wage amount, pay frequency, work schedules, reported Social Security payment and type, defined-benefit pension fields, inheritance expectations, and estate intent. SSA mortality and 2022 program parameters value life-contingent cash flows; Federal Reserve Financial Accounts data provide the defined-benefit pension benchmark.
